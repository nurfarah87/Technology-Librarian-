About Me

Hello! My name is NurFarah Farhana Binti Ismail, and I am a technology enthusiast and professional with a passion for digital innovation. I am currently focusing on Smart Healthcare Hub, where I aim to combine library science and technology to improve access to information.

This website introduces me as both a digital innovator and a committed future-ready librarian. The digital journey to reliable health information begins here—guided by a futuristic Technology Librarian, powered by AI, and designed to support users with speed, accuracy, and confidence.

Equipped with advanced digital skills and human-centered design, this platform provides real-time assistance through AI chatbot interaction, manages health content using modern metadata and classification systems, and enhances user experiences with immersive tools like Virtual Reality (VR) and interactive blogs. Accessibility is ensured with bilingual support and mobile responsiveness.

Ultimately, the Technology Librarian library website represents the future of health libraries: accessible, intelligent, and always ready to support research and wellness needs.
